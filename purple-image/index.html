PURPLE IMAGE

Is an easy to use package of tools designed to speedup the process and the performance in your games. Hevedy Image provides a faster workflow than any other monthly paid complete Image Suite.

FEATURES

The tools provides a complete feature list

Editor

Combine, split and edit the textures on fly, create packaged textures for performance with a single drag and drop.
Take an image of your game, edit the colors and import the generated LUT Table and share the Preset.

Batcher

Package your textures in batch to save load times and boost the performance.

Palette

Pick your colors, make a palette and export it to your favorite draw tools!

Board

Use as reference your images on the desktop and work over.

    OS: Windows 10 (x64) Home or superior
    CPU: 2.8 GHz (+2 Cores, +4 Threads)(SSE4 & AVX2 support) or alike.
    RAM: 4 GB (2 GB Free).
    GPU: DirectX 11 - SM 5.0 Nvidia 660 GTX alike or superior.
    GPU VRAM: 2GB or more.
    Free Disk Space: At least 500 MB (HDD) (SSD Recommended)
