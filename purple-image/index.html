PURPLE IMAGE

A user-friendly suite of tools designed to accelerate your game development workflow. Purple Image offers faster and more efficient texture creation, color selection, and palette management than any other comprehensive image editing software available through monthly subscriptions.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • Whats is this tool for?

    Purple Image is a comprehensive suite of tools designed to speed up your workflow and improve image editing and texture management for game developers, designers, and digital artists.
    It provides a faster and more streamlined experience than many monthly subscription image editing suites.

    Color Selector: Easily pick and create palettes for web design, color grading, or applying filters. The tool helps you build harmonious color schemes adaptable to various projects.

    Batcher: Convert and process entire folders of images automatically. You can package different color channels, batch-edit textures, and optimize them for better performance.

    Board: Use this tool as a virtual desktop for your reference images. It allows you to arrange and view multiple images simultaneously, so you can draw, paint, or design with your inspirations right at hand.

    Editor: Combine, split, and edit textures on the fly. Create packaged textures optimized for performance with simple drag and drop. Take screenshots of your game, adjust colors, import LUT tables, and share presets effortlessly.

  • Does Purple Image depend on other tools?

    No. All tools are built from the ground up and work standalone without dependencies on third-party software.

  • Are the licenses valid for commercial use?

    Official stores include Itch.io and Gumroad only. Both Individual/Personal and Studio/Professional licenses allow commercial use.

    Licenses are DRM-free, royalty-free, platform-independent, and non-expiring.

    If your team or studio has over $100,000 in revenue or funding in the past 12 months, the Studio/Professional license is required.

    All textures, images, noise, and assets generated with the tools may be used commercially, non-commercially, and for educational purposes.

    Redistribution or sublicensing of the tools is prohibited. Licenses grant usage rights to generate content freely.

  • Are versions different across marketplaces?

    No, differences only arise from update timing. Some marketplaces may receive updates earlier than others, but versions are otherwise consistent.

  • What are the minimum system requirements?

    Purple Image requires CPUs supporting SSE4 and AVX2, and GPUs supporting DirectX 11 (SM5 or SM6) or Vulkan on Windows 10+ (64-bit).


    Minimum Specs:

    OS: Windows 10 (x64) Home or superior
    CPU: 2.8 GHz (2+ Cores, 4+ Threads) with SSE4 & AVX2 support.
    RAM: 4 GB (2 GB Free).
    GPU: DirectX 11 - SM 5.0 (e.g., Nvidia GTX 660 or better)
    GPU VRAM: 2GB or more.
    Free Disk Space: Minimum 500 MB (SSD recommended)
