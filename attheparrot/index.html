Frequently Asked Questions

  • Whats this game about?

    Basically you have to survive the night spotting different anomalies that occurs along the night, if you have too many happening at the same time and don't spot them, you lost.

  • Does this game have online or multiplayer?

    Nope, this isn't an option either is planned, but it have remote play.

  • Can interact with friends?

    If you bought the game in Steam you have access to Steam Remote Play feature, allows your friends to play and watch the same game you are playing, outside of Steam we got a custom solution on the game launcher, but it needs portforward in order to connect also is experimental.

  • Need a super computer to run this? / Minimum Specs

    Actually no, using a custom version of Unreal Engine edited by us using custom rendering methods that allow to run it on high framerate at old GPUs, you can check the minimum specs list.
    *ONLY works under DirectX (11 or 12) (SM5 or SM6) on Windows +10 (x64)


    Minimum Specs: (720p or 1080p at 30 FPS)

    OS: Windows 10 (x64) Home or superior
    CPU: 2.8 GHz Intel i5 Series or alike.
    RAM: 6 GB (3 GB Free).
    GPU: DirectX 11 - SM 5.0 Nvidia 960 GTX alike or superior.
    GPU VRAM: 2GB or more.
    Free Disk Space: 5 GB (HDD)


    Recommended Specs: (1080p or 1440p or 2160p (4K Untested))

    OS: Windows 10 (x64) Home or superior
    CPU: 3.5 GHz Intel i5 Series or alike.
    RAM: 8 GB (4 GB Free).
    GPU: DirectX 12 - SM 6.0 Nvidia 2060 GTX alike or superior.
    GPU VRAM: 6GB or more.
    Free Disk Space: 6 GB (SSD)
